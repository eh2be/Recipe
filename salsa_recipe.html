A classic Mexican-style chunky tomato salsa

Ingredients

  • 12 fresh roma tomatoes
  • 1 medium white onion
  • 1 bunch fresh cilantro
  • 2-3 fresh serrano peppers
  • 1 fresh lime
  • 1-tbsp sea salt (to taste)

Method

  1. cut tomatoes lengthwise and remove slimy innards
  2. cut tomatoes into 1/4 inch pieces
  3. chop onion into 1/8 pieces
  4. dice serrano pepper(s)
  5. wash and chop cilantro finely
  6. mix all chopped ingredients in a bowl
  7. add juice from limes and salt to taste
  8. enjoy with good quality tortilla chips
